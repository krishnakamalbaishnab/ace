# ACE — Abroad & Career Essentials

**By Students, For Students — Study Abroad Done Right**

ACE is a modern, student-first consultancy helping students study abroad in top countries like UK, Germany, USA, Canada, Netherlands, and Australia. We provide practical, unbiased support across every step of the study abroad journey.

## 🌟 Why Choose ACE?

- **No University Tie-ups**: We're completely independent. Our recommendations are based on what's best for YOU, not what earns us commissions.
- **We've Been There**: Our team consists of international students who've successfully navigated the study abroad journey.
- **Practical, Unbiased Support**: From applications to accommodation, we provide honest, practical advice based on real experience.
- **24/7 Support**: We understand time zones and deadlines. Our support team is available whenever you need us.

## 🎯 Services We Provide

### Core Services
- **University Shortlisting**: Personalized recommendations based on your profile, budget, and career goals
- **Application Support**: Expert guidance for SOP, Resume, LOR, and all application documents
- **Assignment & Project Mentoring**: Academic support to help you excel in your studies abroad
- **Part-time Job Support**: Help you find suitable part-time work opportunities while studying
- **Accommodation Help**: Assistance in finding safe and affordable accommodation options
- **Visa & Pre-departure**: Complete visa application support and pre-departure guidance

### Study Destinations
- 🇬🇧 **United Kingdom**: World-class universities, rich history, and diverse culture
- 🇩🇪 **Germany**: Free education, strong economy, and innovation hub
- 🇺🇸 **United States**: Innovation capital, diverse programs, and career opportunities
- 🇨🇦 **Canada**: High quality of life, welcoming environment, and PR pathways
- 🇳🇱 **Netherlands**: English-taught programs, bike-friendly cities, and innovation
- 🇦🇺 **Australia**: Beautiful landscapes, quality education, and work opportunities

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Local Development
1. Clone the repository:
   ```bash
   git clone [repository-url]
   cd ace
   ```

2. Open `index.html` in your web browser to view the website locally.

3. For development:
   - Make changes to HTML, CSS, or JavaScript files
   - Test locally before deploying
   - Ensure all links and forms work correctly

### Deployment
The website is configured for deployment on Vercel:

1. Connect your repository to Vercel
2. Vercel will automatically detect the static site
3. Deploy with zero configuration
